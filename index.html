About Us

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based experiential learning organization redefining education through real-world exposure. We design programs that bridge classroom knowledge with practical understanding.

From academic excursions to maker workshops, our initiatives encourage students to learn by doing, think creatively, and collaborate effectively under guided mentorship.
