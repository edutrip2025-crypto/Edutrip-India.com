About Us

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based organization redefining how students learn by connecting classrooms to real-world environments. We design curriculum-aligned journeys that nurture curiosity, creativity and collaboration.

From field based science explorations to heritage residencies and innovation bootcamps, every Edutrip experience is guided by educators and industry mentors. We transform “learning by reading” into “learning by doing.”
