About Us

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based organization transforming education through experiential learning. We connect classroom teaching with real-world exploration, empowering students through structured, interactive programs.

Our initiatives combine curriculum relevance with engaging, hands-on learning — from educational trips and expert-led sessions to maker workshops and innovation bootcamps. We focus on developing curiosity, creativity, and collaboration among learners.
