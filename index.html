About Us

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based experiential learning organization redefining education through real-world experiences. We design curriculum-aligned journeys that nurture curiosity, creativity and collaboration among students.

Our experiences are curated by educators and industry mentors — transforming “learning by reading” into “learning by doing.”
