About Us

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based organization redefining how students learn by connecting classrooms to real-world environments. We design curriculum-aligned journeys that nurture curiosity, creativity, and collaboration among students.

From science explorations and heritage trails to innovation bootcamps, every Edutrip experience is guided by educators and mentors. We transform “learning by reading” into “learning by doing.”
