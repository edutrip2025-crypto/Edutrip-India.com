Our Programs

Transformative experiences designed to connect classroom learning with real-world discovery

01

Curriculum-Linked Trips

Co-designed experiential trips connecting classroom topics to real-world places. Students explore museums, historical sites, science centers, and natural environments while learning alongside expert guides who bring subjects to life through immersive experiences.

02

SimpLearn Sessions

Interactive sessions led by industry experts focusing on STEM and design thinking. Hands-on learning experiences that simplify complex concepts through engaging demonstrations, activities, and real-world problem-solving challenges that inspire curiosity.

03

Workshops & Maker Labs

Hands-on maker labs developing design and prototyping skills. Students work with professional tools, materials, and technologies to create innovative solutions to real-world problems while learning collaboration and creative thinking.

04

Impact & StuCom

Student communication and progress-tracking systems with measurable impact. Comprehensive reporting and analytics help schools understand and showcase the learning outcomes achieved through our experiential programs.

Who We Are

About Edutrip

Redefining education through real-world experiences

Transforming Education Since 2015

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based experiential learning organization redefining education through real-world experiences. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and practical application.

From field explorations to innovation bootcamps and heritage residencies, every Edutrip experience is guided by experienced educators and industry mentors passionate about transforming education.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and develop critical thinking skills in students through immersive, hands-on learning experiences that prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow.
