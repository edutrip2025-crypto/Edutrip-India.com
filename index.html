About Us

Edutrip is a Hyderabad-based experiential learning organisation redefining education through real-world experiences. We design curriculum-aligned journeys that nurture curiosity, creativity, and collaboration among students.

From field science explorations to innovation bootcamps and heritage residencies, every Edutrip experience is guided by educators and mentors. We transform “learning by reading” into “learning by doing.”
